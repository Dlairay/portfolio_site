Experimental AI Tutor

Project Description

This project was for a hackathon, where I tackled the issue of equitable education. I experimented with integrating the most effective study techniques covered in the book "Make It Stick", with chatgpt's and youtube's API. Integrated dynamic urls and content generation loading with Jinja and Flask

My Reflections & Learnings

This was before I learned of RAG, near the start of my coding journey. In hindsight, Its just a glorified chatgpt wrapper, and the hardest part was just figuring out how to generate dynamic urls and webpages that will change depending of what chatgpt generates automatically.
